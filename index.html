Our Story

A Legacy Born
From Heritage

Rooted in the royal kitchens of Rajasthan and refined at the finest tables of Paris, Chef Arjun Mehta founded Zaffran in 2011 with one purpose — to bring India's most extraordinary culinary traditions to the world stage.

Zaffran — named for the world's most precious spice — is a celebration of India's 5,000-year culinary story. Every dish is a bridge between heirloom recipes passed down through generations and contemporary fine dining technique. We source single-estate spices, hand-press our own oils, and slow-cook curries the way maharajas once demanded — with patience, precision, and devotion.

"Indian cuisine is not just food. It is memory, ritual, and love — layered in a single bite."

— Chef Arjun Mehta
